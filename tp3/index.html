Christmas in Strasbourg

Whether you want to take in the must-see highlights or seek out hidden gems, we’ve put together a bespoke itinerary to help you make the most of Strasbourg’s Christmas Markets and their 300 chalets. Explore local crafts, regional produce and traditional Christmas decorations, visit the majestic Cathedral, stroll through the historic Petite France quarter, or pop into one of the city’s many museums.




Martín de las Heras, 2019
